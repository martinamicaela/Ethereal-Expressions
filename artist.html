About Author

Adamma is a British Nigerian Authour, born in one of the most artistic cities in the whole of the UK : Cardiff. The Welsh capital city would be the starting point from where Adamma would complete her studies in Fine Art and would set up her first gallery "Etherial Expressions". With the success of her first gallery Adamma dared to expand her art overseas and it was an absolute success. Part of her succes is linked to Adamma's lifetime experience. Since her childhood Adamma developed a very unique identity, as her family roots, both Welsh and Nigerian allowed her to have a richer knowledge about artistic style and expression. In her first presentation in Paris 2015, Adamma aimed to create something that could represent not only modernity, abstraction and beauty, but also a glipmse of herself:"My art reflcts my heritage, my passion, my present and my future"
